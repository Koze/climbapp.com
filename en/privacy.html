Privacy Policy

Climb App Inc. ("we" or "the Company") sets forth the following policy regarding the handling of user information in our apps and website.

1. Basic Policy

We handle information only to the extent necessary to provide our services. We do not intend to persistently collect or store information that directly identifies users.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you use the contact form on our website:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Message content

This information is used solely for responding to inquiries and will not be provided to third parties except as required by law.

3. Information Collected by Third-Party Services

Our apps may use the following third-party services for feature improvement, usage analysis, and ad delivery:

  • Google Analytics / Google Analytics for Firebase
  • Google AdMob
  • Apple analytics and distribution services

Device information, OS information, advertising identifiers, app usage data, crash data, IP addresses, and similar data may be collected.

4. Purpose of Information Use

  • Providing, maintaining, and improving our services
  • Investigating bugs and improving quality
  • Ad delivery and performance measurement
  • Responding to inquiries

5. User Controls

You can restrict the use of advertising identifiers and change tracking permissions through your device settings.

6. Third-Party Privacy Policies

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • AdMob Policy
  • Apple Privacy Policy

7. Revisions

This policy may be revised as needed. Significant changes will be announced on this page.

8. Contact

Please reach out via our contact form.
